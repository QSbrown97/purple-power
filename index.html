Purple Passion

Why Purple?

For as long as I have been alive, until recently, if you asked me what my favorite color was I would've said Green! Now at the age of 26 I am finally realizing that my favorite color has been Purple all along. Thinking back to my child/teen years, I realized I have always ended up with purple things. Purple toys, Purple clothes, Purple anything! Now I am full on embracing my love for Purple and all its various shades.
